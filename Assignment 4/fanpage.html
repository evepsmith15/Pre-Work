Nippon Ichi's Tenpei Sato!

BIO

Tenpei Sato is a music composer and voice actor. He is most known for producing the music for the Japanese Company Nippon Ichi. Most of his famous works are for the Disgaea series. An interesting tidbit about him is that he started playing piano at the age of six, and later attended Waseda University.
